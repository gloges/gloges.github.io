About Me

I am a Physics graduate student at the University of Wisconsin - Madison, interested in HEP Theory and Mathematical Physics. In May 2017 I graduated from the University of Rochester with a B.S. in Physics and B.S. in Mathematics.

Currently in my first year at UW Madison, I am working as a graduate TA in the Physics Department, teaching General Physics (PHY 104).

I am an avid hiker and recently completed about 275 miles of the Appalachian Trail (AT) with MD in May-June 2017. Many thanks to the thru-hikers we came to know on the trail, and congratulations on your feat (and feet)! You made the time that much more enjoyable.
