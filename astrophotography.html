M81 (Bode's Galaxy) & M82 (Cigar Galaxy)

Images taken 2019/12/10, edited 2020/01/11.

  • Light: (140x) 30s, ISO 800
  • Dark: (30x) 30s, ISO 800
  • Bias: 20x
  • Flat: None

Integration time: 70 min.
Aligned and stacked in DeepSkyStacker, edited in GIMP.

I made my life hard by not taking flats. I tried creating a fake background gradient a few times before arriving at something I was happy with.

M 27 (Dumbbell Nebula)

Images taken 2019/10/27, edited 2019/10/29

  • Light: (50x) 90s, ISO 3200
  • Dark: (10x) 90s, ISO 3200
  • Bias: 10x
  • Flat: None

Integration time: 75 min.
Aligned and stacked in DeepSkyStacker, edited in GIMP.

Taken during my first observation, after having looked at Jupiter and Saturn at dusk. This is before the coma corrector, so I have cropped way in and focussed on bringing out the stark colors.
