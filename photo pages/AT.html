Appalachian Trail, Summer 2017

Pole Breaker and I (Barefoot Cook) did a section of the AT from May 21 to June 19. Given that we were not going for a predetermined distance, we packed heavy and had a single resupply half-way through. Starting just south of Mt. Greylock in Massachussetts, we made it to Crawford Notch, frustratingly shy of Mt. Washington.

Thanks to all of the thru-highers and AT denizens we met and spent time with: Professor T., Tigger, Flip-flopper, Redbud, Whisper (and Boomer!), Tree Walker, The Gatherer, Purple Haze, Downwind, Medieval and Secrets, NOBO Joe, Gandalf and Flicker, Quin, Shark Bait, Ready, Hamp, Firefly, Beast, Peace Walker, Yes-Man, Long Haul, Snap, Butt-Tape and Dilly-Dally, Fax Machine, Dr. Gadget, Crumbles and Big Beef, Zen Master, Red Pill, Yogi, Fifty, Web Catcher.
